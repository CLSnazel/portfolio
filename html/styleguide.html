Left+Right Classes

Since the columns will center themselves within their individual containers, the content gets pushed further into the middle of the page. This causes an awkward misalignment of content, and to solve this the "left" class is applied to the left-most item in the column section, and "right" applied to the right-most item. This works for both 2 and 3 column sets
